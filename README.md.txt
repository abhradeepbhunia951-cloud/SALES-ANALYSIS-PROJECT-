Customer Sales Analysis Projects

Objective:
To analyze customer and sales data to understand sales trends, top products, and  customer behaviour.

Dataset:
- customers.csv
- sales.csv

Tools used:
- Python(Pandas, matplotlib)
- Excel

Key Insights:
1. A small group of customers contributes a large portion of total revenue.
2. Sales show seasonal trends with higher revenue in certain months.
3.A few products generate most of the overall sales.
4. Major cities contributes higher sales compared to others.

Dashboard:
The Dashboard shows total revenue, total orders, monthly sales trends, top products, and city-wise sales performances.
